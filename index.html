Overview

The aftermath of a Computer malware attack can cause billions of dollars in damage. Malware such as trojans can disguise themselves to stay hidden on your computer while stealing sensitive information. 32% of the world’s computers are infected with some type of malware, however, an even higher percentage of people don’t check. ref

The term malware comes from malicious software. Malware is malicious code taking the form of an innocent program or file designed to control or alterthe way your computer works. There are many different types of malware butthe most common are viruses, worms, trojans and ransomware. No matter what malware you come across, it is important to know what steps you need to take in order to remove it because the outcome will always be the same. The intentions behind malware usually fall between the lines of demanding ransom or to steal sensitive personal data. ref

It would be more effective to put money into advertising malware prevention rather than spending it to cover the costs of the damages.
Many people don’t realise how dangerous malware is and how you are puttingyour personal data at risk if such malware gains access into your computer.If people are more aware of how these threats enter our computers then theamount of money spent on damage control and stolen data will be reduced inthe future. ref

I have decided to design an interactive website called .exe which will help a range of audiences to learn about computer malware. The website willinform them on how dangerous malware is and how to avoid it to preventdamages and stolen information. I will present this infromation in the formof an infographic on my website allowing my audience to intake plenty of information in as few words as possible

Reflection

When I first read the brief for this project I wasnt sure which direction I wanted to go with. I wanted to do something pysical but thought that my prototype would be presented better on a digital platform.

I am a little bit shocked how my website turned out becasue I wasn't expecting it to turn out the way it did. I had something else in mind but this isn't a negative as I am still happy with the result. Usuaully when I make websites I stick to the standard clean and pressional looking layout but this time I went out of my comfort zone and desgined a more compacted and messy website. The messy looked suits the topic of computer malware as they are very destructive. I feel like my website is more appealing for my audience to look and interact with being it isnt your typical blank page and text.

I did struggle a lot with this design style because there were many clashes in elements especially when I was layering the text with shapes. Sometimes objects would look off centre and the colour scheme didn't go well with each other. I had to play around with many different combinations of colours and elements to get the right look.

Overall my website displays the messy minimalist aesthetic I was going for and I am happy that I got all the functionality that I needed to work because people can now gain the full experience of whats to come for my website.

Presentaton

Here is a summary of my presentation done in the media lab

WHAT IS IT

My prototype is a website. The way I would like to present this to a wider audience or publicize it is through email spams. It may be ironic because the whole purpose is to avoid dangerous emails but I think it would be an effective way for me to get my narrow down the more vulnerable audiences.

WHO IS IT FOR

My website is for anyone who uses the internet. It is primarirly targetted towards those who have little to no knowledge about computer malware or want to learn more about this topic. I can see this website being used in schooling for children so they are more aware of these threats at a younger age.

WHAT WILL IT DO

my website will promote internet safety for all internet users by providing them with important information on computer malware. This includes how you get it, how it spreads and how to prevent it. I also have multiple signs and interactives that link to infecting your computer with malware. These include the first load up page, it is a warning page for people to retreat but if they don't they may infect their computers. The other one is an online interative where pop up ads and warnings will come up if you tap on the risky links. All these are created for the purpose of familiarising my target audiences of how and where these threats hide.
